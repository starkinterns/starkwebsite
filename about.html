Our Story

Built on Innovation & Trust

STARK TECHNOVATIONS is an Eswatini-based technology company specializing in electronic security systems, ICT infrastructure, software development, cloud services, fire safety systems, and power backup solutions.

We provide reliable, scalable, and innovative solutions for businesses, institutions, and residential clients throughout Southern Africa. Our approach combines technical expertise, quality workmanship, and customer-focused support to deliver solutions that protect, connect, and empower our clients.
